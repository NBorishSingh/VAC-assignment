About

The FN FAL is a battle rifle developed in Belgium and used by many countries during the Cold War. It became known as one of the most widely used military rifles of the 20th century.

History

  • Developed after World War II
  • Used widely during the Cold War
  • Adopted by over 90 countries
  • Known for durability
