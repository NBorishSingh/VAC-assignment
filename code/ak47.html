About

The AK-47 is a gas-operated assault rifle developed in the Soviet Union by Mikhail Kalashnikov. It became one of the most widely used rifles in the world due to its durability and reliability.

History

  • Designed in 1947
  • Entered service in 1949
  • Widely used during the Cold War
  • Produced in many countries worldwide

Specifications

Specification Details
Type Assault Rifle
Country Soviet Union
Designer Mikhail Kalashnikov
Caliber 7.62×39mm
Magazine 30 rounds
Effective Range 300–400 meters

Key Features

  • Gas-operated firing system
  • High reliability in harsh conditions
  • Simple mechanical design
  • Durable construction
  • Easy maintenance

Interesting Facts

  • The AK-47 is one of the most produced rifles in history.
  • Millions of units have been manufactured worldwide.
  • It is known for functioning even in extreme environments.
